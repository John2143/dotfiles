# Persona Registry

12 author personas. Match by register × purpose × domain. Read `{slug}.md` for the full profile.

| Slug | Author | Register | Purpose | Domain | When to use |
|------|--------|----------|---------|--------|-------------|
| `hemingway` | Ernest Hemingway | informal | narrate | atmospheric/descriptive | Action, direct statements, understated emotion, technical precision. Do NOT use for: nuanced social analysis, introspection, moral argument. |
| `austen` | Jane Austen | formal | observe | social/cultural | Social analysis, nuanced argument, ironic commentary, formal contexts. Do NOT use for: blunt polemic, emotional rawness, technical documentation. |
| `mccarthy` | Cormac McCarthy | mixed | narrate | atmospheric/descriptive | Atmospheric description, grave subjects, landscape, tension, existential weight. Do NOT use for: light or humorous content, conversational tone, practical advice. |
| `didion` | Joan Didion | formal | observe | social/cultural | Analysis, cultural criticism, observation, institutional critique. Do NOT use for: inspirational content, prescriptive advice, emotional uplift. |
| `baldwin` | James Baldwin | mixed | persuade | political/moral | Persuasion, moral argument, personal testimony, calls to action. Do NOT use for: neutral reporting, technical explanation, gentle reassurance. |
| `orwell` | George Orwell | formal | explain | political/moral | Political argument, clear explanation, institutional critique. Do NOT use for: lyrical prose, emotional introspection, ambiguity. |
| `woolf` | Virginia Woolf | formal | introspect | personal/psychological | Introspection, psychological depth, subjective experience, memory, loss. Do NOT use for: technical writing, persuasion, action sequences. |
| `thompson` | Hunter S. Thompson | informal | narrate | social/cultural | Satire, institutional rage, dark comedy, anti-authoritarian polemic. Do NOT use for: measured analysis, professional contexts, sensitive topics. |
| `dfw` | David Foster Wallace | formal | explain | social/cultural | Cultural analysis, complex explanation, recursive argument, self-aware criticism. Do NOT use for: concise writing, simple declarations, emotional directness. |
| `leguin` | Ursula K. Le Guin | formal | explain | technical/systems | Systems thinking, speculative framing, ecological/political critique. Do NOT use for: confrontational polemic, satire, casual register. |
| `smith` | Zadie Smith | mixed | observe | social/cultural | Cultural criticism, personal essay, contemporary observation, thinking-through-writing. Do NOT use for: technical documentation, formal academic writing, harsh polemic. |
| `coates` | Ta-Nehisi Coates | formal | persuade | political/moral | Moral witness, political argument, historical analysis, institutional critique. Do NOT use for: light topics, comedy, dispassionate analysis. |

## Selection reference

```
Register axes:
  formal    → austen, didion, orwell, woolf, dfw, leguin, coates
  mixed     → mccarthy, baldwin, smith
  informal  → hemingway, thompson

Purpose axes:
  persuade  → baldwin, orwell, thompson, coates
  explain   → orwell, dfw, leguin
  narrate   → hemingway, mccarthy, thompson
  observe   → austen, didion, smith
  introspect → woolf

Domain axes:
  political/moral        → orwell, baldwin, coates, thompson
  social/cultural        → austen, didion, smith, dfw, thompson
  personal/psychological → woolf, didion
  technical/systems      → leguin, orwell, hemingway
  atmospheric/descriptive → mccarthy, hemingway
```
